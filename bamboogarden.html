Final Installation
A public space is a social space that is generally open accessible to people. The Bamboo Garden at the Brown University is a public space, yet it encompasses a distinct privacy that presents tranquility and peacefulness to the visitors. The installation emphasizes the relationship between the publicity and privacy of the site, allowing an individual experience under an exposed setup.
